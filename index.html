AI Voice Booking Agent

n8n VAPI OpenAI Google Calendar

Appointment-Based Services · Salons, Clinics, Consultants

The Challenge

Service businesses were bleeding revenue through missed calls and manual booking chaos. Staff spent 20+ hours weekly playing phone tag. After-hours calls went to voicemail, and frustrated customers booked with competitors instead.

The Solution

Built an AI-powered voice booking system using VAPI that handles calls 24/7. It greets callers, checks real-time Google Calendar availability, answers service questions, and books appointments instantly. n8n triggers SMS/email confirmations and CRM sync.

90-100% reduction in manual scheduling
24/7 booking increased revenue by 28%
Zero missed calls
Satisfaction jumped to 4.7 stars
20+ hours/week freed
